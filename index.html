How the Tournament Works
  • One-time accountCreate your username and password once. It’s used for every future tournament, so your record follows you.
  • Invitation onlyWhen a new bracket is announced, invited players receive a link to join.
  • Pick your partnerAfter accepting the invite, you choose your partner. They confirm and the team locks in.
  • First to 250Each match is scored to 250 under the house rules below.
House Rules
  • Every hand bids itselfEach player bids their own hand. Team bid = Player A + Player B bids.
  • Ace highRanking: A, K, Q, J, 10 ... 2. No jokers.
  • Open & closed biddingHost decides if bids are shown or hidden until all are in.
  • No NilEvery player must bid at least one book.
  • No over pointsOnly earn points for what you bid — no bags.
  • Spade stick to the tableYou must follow suit. Spades can’t be led until broken.
  • Don’t force a renegeThe system prevents illegal plays.
  • The Yard — 10 booksCalling 10 books earns +20 if successful, or a 200-point penalty if failed.
  • Win conditionFirst team to reach 250 points wins.
